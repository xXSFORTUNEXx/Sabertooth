Keyboard Controls
W - Move Up
A - Move Left
S - Move Down
D - Move Right

I - Look Up
J - Look Left
K - Look Down
L - Look Right

Space - Shoot Weapon
O - Pickup Item
R - Reload
E - Interact

C - Chat Window
B - Debug Window
M - User Interface

Xbox Controls
Left Stick - Move
Right Stick - Aim
Left Trigger - Shoot Weapon
A - Pickup
X - Reload
B - Interact
